# Multidimensional Analysis of Stock Trading: The Market Perspective

This document outlines the analytical frameworks applied when treating **Stocks (Equities)** as the primary entities within the TLU physics engine. In this perspective, the market itself is the organism, and users are the external forces or fluid flowing through it.

## 1. Thermodynamic Analysis (Liquidity & Volatility)
Treating a stock as a thermodynamic system allows us to measure its "heat" and "energy."
- **Heat (Dissipation):** Corresponds to Gross Trading Volume. High-frequency trading or wash trading generates massive heat without fundamentally changing the stock's intrinsic value (Work).
- **Entropy (Chaos):** Corresponds to price volatility and the unpredictability of order flow. A highly entropic stock is chaotic, speculative, and lacks consensus.
- **Free Energy:** Represents the stock's remaining liquidity and capacity to absorb massive buy/sell shocks without collapsing in price. When Free Energy plummets, the stock is highly vulnerable to flash crashes.

## 2. Structural Mechanics (Resilience & Correlation)
Treating stocks as physical structures connected by springs (user capital flows) reveals the market's rigidity.
- **Structural Stiffness Matrix:** Measures how tightly a stock is coupled to others. If Stock A crashes, how much "mechanical stress" is transferred to Stock B because they share the exact same investor base?
- **Principal Component Axes (Eigen-Analysis):** 
  - **PC1 Dominance (The Market Vector):** If a stock's variance is 90% explained by PC1, it moves entirely with the macroeconomic tide (e.g., an ETF or mega-cap).
  - **PC2+ Dominance (Idiosyncratic Noise):** If higher-order components dominate, the stock is isolated from the macro market and driven by retail frenzies or sector-specific news.

## 3. Control Theory (Systemic Risk & Market Manipulation)
Applying control theory to the bipartite network reveals hidden feedback loops and systemic vulnerabilities.
- **Spectral Radius (Wash Trading):** If the transaction network surrounding a stock forms a closed loop, the Spectral Radius mathematically converges to `1.0`. This is the undeniable physical signature of artificial volume inflation (Wash Trading).
- **Sensitivity Matrix:** Identifies "Keystone Equities." If trading for this specific stock is halted, the sensitivity matrix calculates the exact cascading liquidity crisis that will impact the rest of the market.
